01. Semantic Drift

We don't search for banned words. The `all-MiniLM-L6-v2` engine projects every multi-step agent action into a mathematical coordinate. We calculate the cosine distance against your custom threat heuristics. If the intent drifts too far, it triggers a fatal block.
